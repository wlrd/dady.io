ingredients

daal

  • 1 cup daal
  • 5 cup water
  • 4 pieces garlic
  • .25 tsp haldi
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1.5 tsp crush pepper
  • 1/4 bunch of cilantro

tarka

  • some minced garlic
  • little bit of oil
  • 1/4 tsp cumin

steps

  • put daal, water, garlic, haldi, salt, crush pepper in pressure cooker. medium heat until pressure goes off
  • pressure for 15 minutes. stop head let the pressure guy chill. take cap off. cook water off
  • in a seperate pan do the tarka by putting some garlic in a little oil. once goldenish add cumin
  • drop the tarka guy into the pressure cooker
  • if you are feeling a lil chef guy, chop up cilantro and garnish
