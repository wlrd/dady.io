ingredients

  • 1 eggplant medium(make sure heavy)
  • 1 medium sized onion
  • 4-5 green chillis
  • 2 small tomatoes
  • 1/3 cup canola oil
  • .5 tsp zeera
  • .5 tsp salt
  • .25 tsp haldi
  • 1 tsp crushed pepper
  • 1 lemon for juice
  • 3 aloos

steps

  • cut onions into small thin slices
  • put oil and onions into pan. put on medium heat
  • cut up green chillies into small pieces and tomatoes into small cubes
  • peel eggplant and chop into little slices
  • when onions look transparent add tomatoes, green chillies and zeera
  • cut aloo into cubes
  • add haldi,crushed peppers, salt when tomatoes are mushed. mix momy
  • cook for one minute
  • add aloo and cook for 2 minutes
  • add eggplant. stir every few minutes. cover and cook on medium heat
  • move to low heat after 10 minutes cooked.
  • add lemon when aloo are almost soft
  • stop when aloo are soft
